Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at Vivanta Pune, Hinjawadi

Situated 2.1 km from Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park in Hinjewadi, Vivanta Pune Hinjawadi features a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel has an outdoor pool and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the snack bar. Free private parking is available on site.
Each room is fitted with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find bath robes and slippers.
Car hire is available at this hotel and the area is popular for golfing.
Tata Technologies, KPIT Infotech, Accenture are within 3 km of the property while Tata Consultancy Services, Tech Mahindra, Volkswagen India and Barclays are within 10 km distance. University of Pune is 12 km from Vivanta Pune Hinjawadi and Fergusson College is 15 km away. Pune Railway station is 20 km away while the nearest airport is Pune International Airport, 21 km from Vivanta Pune Hinjawadi.

Vivanta Pune, Hinjawadi has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 22 Sept 2015.
