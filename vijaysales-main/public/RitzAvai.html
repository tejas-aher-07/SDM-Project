Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at The Ritz-Carlton, Pune

Set in Pune, 4 km from Pune International Airport The Ritz-Carlton, Pune offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. With free WiFi, this 5-star hotel has a bar and a terrace. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.
All guest rooms come with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, a hairdryer and a desk. At the hotel all rooms have a wardrobe and a private bathroom.
Guests at The Ritz-Carlton, Pune can enjoy a continental or a buffet breakfast.
The accommodation offers a sauna. Bike hire and car hire are available at The Ritz-Carlton, Pune and the area is popular for hiking and cycling.
Osho Ashram is 2.5 km from the hotel, while Darshan Museum is 4.1 km from the property. The Poona Club Golf Course and Poonawalla Business Bay are 100 m away, and Koregaon Park is 1.5 km from the property. World Trade Center and EON IT Park are about 6 km away.
